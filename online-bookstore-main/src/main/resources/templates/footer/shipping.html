Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Methods

We offer several shipping options to cater to your needs.

  • Standard Shipping:

    Our Standard Shipping option delivers your books within 3 to 7 business days from the date of purchase. This economical method ensures reliable delivery to your doorstep, providing an affordable shipping solution for all your book orders.

    Estimated Delivery Time: 3-7 business days

    Cost: Calculated based on package weight and destination

  • Expedited Shipping:

    Opt for Expedited Shipping to receive your books sooner! With delivery typically within 1 to 3 business days from the purchase date, this method ensures a faster shipping experience for those eager to dive into their new reads right away.

    Estimated Delivery Time: 1-3 business days

    Cost: Calculated based on package weight and destination

  • Free Shipping (Orders over 99$):

    Enjoy Free Shipping on all orders exceeding $99! Simply reach the qualifying amount in your cart to benefit from this cost-saving option. Receive your books conveniently without worrying about extra shipping fees.

    Estimated Delivery Time: Same as Standard Shipping

  • Same-Day or Next-Day Delivery (Limited Areas):

    For select areas, we offer Same-Day or Next-Day Delivery services to get your books to you at lightning speed. Place your order before a specified cutoff time to receive your books on the same day or the next business day.

    Available in: Limited areas

    Estimated Delivery Time: Same day or next business day

    Cost: Calculated based on package weight and delivery speed

  • International Shipping:

    No matter where you are in the world, our International Shipping option ensures your favorite books reach you. Please note that delivery times may vary depending on the destination country's customs clearance and local postal services.

    Estimated Delivery Time: Varies by destination

    Cost: Calculated based on package weight and destination country

  • Click-and-Collect (In-Store Pickup):

    Prefer to pick up your books at a nearby physical store? Choose the Click-and-Collect option during checkout, and we'll have your order ready for pickup at your convenience.

    Available at: Select physical store locations

    Estimated Pickup Time: Typically within 24-48 hours after order confirmation

Returns Policy

To be eligible for a return, your book must be in the same condition that you received it. It should be fairly unused and in its original packaging.

We accept returns within 90 days of the delivery date. If BookHaven made an error with your order or if the book is defective, we will cover return shipping costs. Otherwise, return shipping will be the responsibility of the customer.

Returns Process

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and details about the book you wish to return. We will guide you through the return process and provide you with a return shipping label, if applicable.

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, we will process the refund to your original method of payment.
